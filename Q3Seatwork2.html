Cookie Background Information

Sea Fairy Cookie

She is a legendary cookie rules over the sea and is often considered to be one with the ocean. Physically and metophorically, Sea Fair Cookie has full control over the gazing seas. She spends her days yearning for Moonlight Cookie waiting for the day when she can finally meet her. Often considered as the "Sea Goddess" herself she sets aside her time as an elemental guardian to gaze over the dimlight of the moon.


TYPE: BOMBER POSITION: MIDDLE PRONOUNS: SHE/HER

Squid Ink Cookie

A Giant Squid who lursk in the deep sea of the Tropical Soda Isalnds. Don't be fooled by their clueless demeanor for Squid Ink Cookie holds great power and is rumored to be a legendary treasure-eating cookie who has attacked numerous ships for It's prized possesions. Although It is not confirmed by anyone to be true, They can't recall much about their own past and passes the time by oozing blackish inky tears.

Sorbet Shark Cookie

A friendly and curious shark memperson is Sorbet Shark Cookie whose dream is to go on an epic voyage with pirates. Being able to transform between their full fishy form and a fun-sized land form, they mostly spend their days swimming the vast ocean waters in search of adventure and unintelligibly babbling bubble-speak on land with the other cookies.


TYPE: AMBUSH POSITION: MIDDLE PRONOUNS: THEY/THEM
